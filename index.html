What is Tune Free, and what is its goal?

Tune Free is a Website developed to show a diverse set of tracks or musics so people can discover new songs which are set to be copyright free for youtube videos, meaning that it can be used to create youtube videos without having trouble with copyright issues.

All musics are copyright free?

The musics displayed on the website are based of videos on youtube which use the songs, or musics that are proven to be free to use on the YouTube platform, meaning that while the musics are basically free, it's not 100% copyright free proof and can have side effects, like it can be used on a video but it doesnt give monetary funds in exchange, as an example.

The tracks are avaiable for download?

Tune Free displays multiple musics from different types of categories, but it shows only a piece of the track and it doesnt have the tracks avaiable for download, as its a small project and full songs would be heavy to load and store.
